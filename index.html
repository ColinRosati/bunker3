parasitic research index
▨

Bioeconomics relates to the movement of $$$ with biological life ▢ This economic link between ecology and monetary value can quantified and studied ▢

The scope of this index looks at invasive speices as a trace of global trade ▢

parasitism is a relationship between speicies▢ The parasite uses a host for food or other gains ▢

The traces of global trade can be measured in many ways ▢ From carbon emissions, language exchange, economic shifts as well as through the impacts on biology ▢

The implications of global trade have significant effects on the movement of biology on earth ▢ Begining with the Spanish empire's movement of boats across the world, i look to the biological parasites that came with this movement▢ The infrastructure of global trade that have expanded into the 21st century benefits more the just humans ▢ Parasitic species adapt to new conditions of the host and their global movements ▢

This index is a work that speculates about causal relationships with global trade and deterministic maps of data visualization▢
